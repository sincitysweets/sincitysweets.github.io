About Us

Fine Chocolates of Las Vegas is a proud family-run business that has been operating Las Vegas since 2005. Our little chocolate factory believes in the traditional style of panning to coat delicious nuts, coffee beans, and dried fruits in delectable chocolate. With over 10 years of experience, we have perfected the hand-ladling task of panning, and we know you'll love our wide selection of special chocolate treats.
